About Us

Founded 2 years ago, Paws&Claws focuses on the well-being and happiness of strays and abandoned pets.

Mainly focused on Dogs and Cats, our shelter provides a lavish life for these pets until they find their true, lovely and permanent home. Our pets are surrounded by caring and lovely staffs who take care of these pets in every aspect possible. We are an open shelter where everyone can donate and visit our pets.

To those who are looking for a best buddy be it dogs, cats or both, Paws&Claws may be the perfect place for you!
