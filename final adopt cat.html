Process for the adoption

Before adopting a pet, some criteria need to be cross checked. Make sure that you are meeting all the requirements listed below in order to provide a comfortable and safe environment for your pets.

Requirements we want from someone before adopting a cat?

The lifeline of a cat is really long, they can live about 15-20 years so it is a huge responsibility and commitment. You should consider the cat as a member odf your family.

  • Minimum 2 meals a day
  • Clean and fresh bowl of water
  • Vet appointment when cat is sick
  • Give care, love and affection
  • Play with them
  • Need to have a littler where your can go pee or poo

Adoption is a very responsible and committing task for the next long years coming. So you need to think carefully before taking this major steps.

Where to adopt and What to bring?

You can adopt on our website PAWS & CLAWS by filling a form or you can also contact us on our number. Our shelter is at WayGan, Mauritius.


We are open on Monday-Saturday at 8:30am-5:00pm for adoption or a visit, and for Sundays and public holidays at 8:30am-13:00pm. Note: Please come at least 1h30min before closing so that ou can choose your buddy without any problems.


It is also important to bring your ID or passport, also a form will be provide to you to fill it if not yet fill and Rs150 payment need to be done for adoption.Our kittens have at least one vaccination done. We will give you instructions on feeding times and what food to give.
