Process for the adoption

Before adopting a pet, some criteria need to be cross checked. Make sure that you are meeting all the requirements listed below in order to provide a comfortable and safe environment for your pets.

Requirements we want from someone before adopting a dog?

We want that you treat the pet well such as don’t chain your dog instead treat them as a member of your family. You must also provide them.

  • Minimum 2 meals a day
  • Clean and fresh bowl of water
  • Vet appointment when dog is sick
  • Give care, love and affection
  • Play with them

    • Adoption is a very responsible and committing task for the next long years coming. So you need to think carefully before taking this major steps.

    Advice when choosing your buddy!

    When going to task the step of adopting a dog you must think first of the way you live. If you will be free for the dog when needed such as taking the dog for a walk often.Therefore, you can choose an energetic buddy. If you are a more relaxing person, a low-energy dog or a senior dog will be advice. If you have cats, you should choose dogs that like cats.But don’t worry we will help you when choosing your buddy as we have many gorgeous,smiling lovers waiting only you to take them.

    Where to adopt and What to bring?

    You can adopt on our website PAWS & CLAWS by filling a form or you can also contact us on our number. Our shelter is at WayGan, Mauritius.


    We are open on Monday-Saturday at 8:30am-5:00pm for adoption or a visit, and for Sundays and public holidays at 8:30am-13:00pm. Note: Please come at least 1h30min before closing so that ou can choose your buddy without any problems.


    It is also important to bring your ID or passport, also a form will be provide to you to fill it if not yet fill and Rs150 payment need to be done for adoption.Our puppies have at least one vaccination done. We will give you instructions on feeding times and what food to give.


    You can also adopt a dog even if you leave in another country, you need a minimum period of 21 days to wait the dog to fly because of rabies vaccine. Concerning, abroad people adoption we will be very strict on the ways and procedures.
